How to Keep Your PC Fast and Secure

Keep your PC running smoothly with these simple steps:

1. Uninstall bloat: Remove programs you don’t use and tidy startup apps.

2. Update regularly: Windows updates, drivers, and software patches.

3. Use antivirus: Enable real‑time protection and schedule weekly scans.

4. Add more RAM/SSD: Hardware upgrades give the biggest performance gains.

5. Backup important data: Use cloud or external drives, test restores.
